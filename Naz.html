Nar & az - Pallas Cats

Learn about these adorable Pallas cats.

Nar

Nar is cagemates with az. they share a simmilar bond like bol and polly. nar can be identified by his often upset look. almost as if he is frowning. he can also be found by his very fluffy tail.

Bol

Az is the daughter of bol & polly. just like her father she can be seen on her log, just like bol and his stump. though she doesnt keep bols grumpy face. she can be located by her long but not fluffy coat. as well as leaning over her log.
